About EVENT
“ अंतः अस्ति प्रारंभः ”

Hailing from the land of rich ancient Sanskrit wisdom, we fathomed that every edge is the beginning of a new horizon that is explorable. The pandemic made the past year lamentable to mankind in numerous ways. It was the end of many things, though it paved the way for limitless things we previously didn’t traverse. Ultimately it’s time to walk down that aspiring route.
TEDxMITAOE is enthralled to reveal the theme of our sophomore edition -"PRARAMBH"
Impelled by the prevailing circumstances and longing for the resurrection of new opportunities, innovations, and ideologies, we have exclusively hand-picked PRARAMBH as our theme for this year.

About TEDxMITAOE:bIs an independent TED event organized in MIT Academy of Engineering, Pune, an autonomous institute affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. This platform was created as a platform to promote TED’s vision of ‘Ideas Worth Spreading’, on a local basis. This year we are organizing our Sophomore Edition under the theme ‘PRARAMBH’. The event will be held in the offline mode, and will feature 6 speakers and 3 performers.
About TED: Is an annual event that brings together the world's best thinkers and doers to present their ideas that matter in any area – Technology, Entertainment, Design, science, humanities, business, and development. A TED event isn't just about business, technology, or entrepreneurship. Diversity of topics is the key. TEDx is an open-source platform committed to spreading ideas worth sharing. Thus we are in a quest to identify potential speakers who help us upheld the moxie of "ideas worth spreading".
